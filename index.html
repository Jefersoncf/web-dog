services

what we provide

dog walking

Yes, your furry one likes to smell new places, identify who has been there and mark the territory. For this, the dog needs to walk. With these daily walks, you allow your dog to know everything through his sense of smell, discovering a new world outside the home!

pet sitting

We take care of your pet in case you need to travel, work or resolve any emergency. We have excellent professionals, and you can count on this special service that guarantees the well-being of your pet while you are away.

overnight care

We have suitable places to take care of your pet during the night. With comfortable houses, professionals taking care and giving him a stroller during the night, where he will sleep peacefully in his very comfortable bed

healthcare

More affection and less costs. When pampering your best friend, you have exclusive discounts on services offered by our partners. Just identify yourself and have access to a series of benefits, from food to specialized services
